Collaboration Current Membership

Hadspec is an international collaboration of scientists at Universities and Research Institutes. Within the United States, the collaboration is centered around Jefferson Lab where members work within the Theory Center. European collaborators are located in Cambridge, Dublin and Edinburgh. The collaboration supports graduate students seeking PhDs in the application of lattice QCD to problems in hadron physics.
